ABOUT US

Mark Salon & Spa was established by acclaimed businessmen Riyaz Nagani and Levan Ramishvilli in April 2013 with the aim to bring world class luxury grooming to Mumbai and India. In partnership with the Dessange, they launched two iconic and statuesque Dessange Paris salons in Mumbai at Kemps Corner and Bandra in September 2013. Dessange Paris is the No.1 “Haute Coiffure” brand in the world and is the leading international chain of luxury salons and spa brand with over 2000 outlets in 80 countries. Femininity, elegance and charm are the three pillars that define the work of Dessange Paris. It is a house of luxury dedicated to women’s beauty across borders with a state of the art, global concept of natural beauty that comes with a promise of tailor-made offerings.

The success of both salons in less than 2 years saw the company rise to the ranks within the market to become a leader and Mark Salon & Spa launched its third Dessange Paris outlet in the plush Phoenix Mills Complex of Lower Parel in May 2015. Mark Salon & Spa now looks to repeat their success with Dessange Paris in Hyderabad, Pune, Chennai and Bangalore amongst other cities. Mark Salon & Spa now looks to bring another iconic international brand Camille Albane Paris to target the mid luxury segment in the grooming market. Camille Albane, today, is present is 14 countries with over 500 salons.

With a vibrant team of employees and headquartered in Mumbai, Mark Salon & Spa is growing from strength to strength in the industry. Levan Ramishvilli, Director, Mark Salon & Spa brings with him over 25 years of international work experience panning markets like France, Georgia, Italy, USA and now India. Riyaz Nagani, Managing Director & CEO, is one of the reputed businessmen of Mumbai with experience panning industries such as Food Industry and Luxury Industry. Together they aim to revolutionize the standards of beauty and grooming in India with state of the art infrastructure, hygiene and world class expertise.
