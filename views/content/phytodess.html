ABOUT PHYTODESS

Launched in 1996, Phytodess products symbolize the alliance of plants, PHYTO, and hair expertise of a large luxury company, DESSANGE. This name symbolizes the foundations of its line of quality scalp and hair care. Phytodess has been the first line of hair products created by a hair salon company. True hair salon artists know: "There is no beautiful hair without a healthy scalp". That is why Phytodess is a genuine hair care brand, designed by professionals for professionals who deliver services and expert advice to clients with high standards in search of products that meet their expectations. The Phytodess philosophy is based on several values: primarily natural ingredients, responsible sourcing fostering respect for biodiversity and the development of the local economy, commitment to the international movement 1% for the Planet, packaging from cardboard from managed forests, and the products and packaging materials tested and validated by the trainers from the DESSANGE Training Center.
