Overview

Mark Salon & Spa was established by acclaimed businessmen Riyaz Nagani and Levan Ramishvilli in April 2013.

The aim was to bring
world class luxury grooming
to Mumbai and India

In partnership with Dessange, they launched two iconic and statuesque Dessange Paris salons in Mumbai at Kemps Corner and Bandra in September 2013.

Dessange Paris is the No.1 "Haute Coiffure" brand in the world and is the leading international chain of luxury salons and spa brand with over 2000 outlets in 80 countries. Feminity, elegance and charm are the three pillars that define the work of Dessange Paris.
